# Mora
A simple, offline‑friendly tool to help in language learning. The goal is to make studying easy, flexible, and accessible without any account walls or similar restrictions.

This app lets you build your own decks, fill them with words you want to learn, and study them through different interactive exercises. Everything stays on your device, giving you full control over your learning.

**Site:** https://myr-g.github.io/Mora/

## What You Can Do
### Deck Management
- Create, rename, and delete decks

- View all decks at a glance

- Card counters for each deck

- Due‑card indicator (for SRS)

### Card Management
- Add new cards

- Edit or delete existing cards

- Search within a deck to quickly find specific cards

### Study Modes
- Review Flashcards — simple front/back review

- SRS Flashcards — spaced repetition scheduling

- Multiple Choice

- Matching (drag & drop)

- Write the Definition

- Reverse Mode (flips the front and back of cards, study from your language → target language)

### Import & Export
- Export all decks at once

- Import to replace your current decks

- Useful for backups or moving between devices

### Interface & Experience
- Light & dark themes
  
- Deck and card‑level due counters (subtle glow around them)

- Fully offline‑friendly

- Installable as a PWA (mobile + desktop)
